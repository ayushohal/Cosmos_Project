Then you take the 2nd group where you first set up all the ICincluded dimensions from the 1st group  and then the remaining dimensions from the 1st group in 2nd group. This was parallel placement of panels would be achieved. Now if the 2nd group of opposites is still has remainimg or left dimensions then we will normally calculate the panels for that.
